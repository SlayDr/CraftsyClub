Safety Guidelines ⚠️

Please read carefully before registering your child.

🛡️

Parent & Guardian Safety Notice

All classes are conducted online via Zoom. Please review the following safety requirements.

🔥

Hot Glue Gun

A hot glue gun will be used in some projects. This tool reaches very high temperatures and can cause burns if mishandled.

✂️

Scissors

Sharp scissors are required for cutting yarn, ribbons and other materials. Adult supervision is essential at all times.

🪡

Needles & Sharp Tools

Some projects involve needles or sharp craft tools for threading beads and securing materials.

👁️

Small Parts & Beads

Small beads and craft pieces are used. Keep away from very young children and pets. Choking hazard.

📋 Requirements for All Students

  • ✦A responsible adult (parent or guardian) must be present and supervising for the full 2-hour session.
  • ✦The adult must handle or directly assist with the hot glue gun, scissors and any sharp tools at all times.
  • ✦CraftsyClub is not liable for any injuries, accidents or damages that occur in the participant's home during class.
  • ✦By registering, parents and guardians accept full responsibility for their child's safety during all sessions.
